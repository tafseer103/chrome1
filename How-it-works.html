How it works

Read to book an experience to remember? Here is how it works...
  1. Choose an experience and place your order through our secure checkout system.
  2. Receive your confirmation of purchase and printable PDF Gift Voucher straight to your email.
  3. If your voucher is a gift, you can now forward them the email or print off the PD and pop it into an envelope for them. If the experience is for yourself, you can move straight on to step 4!
  4. It's time to book your experience! Head to the 'My Voucher' page on this website, and chose a venue and date that suits you, then use the contact details available to book your day.
